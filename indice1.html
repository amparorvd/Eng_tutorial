Aspects about the objectives of teaching English in this basic manual:

- Promote communication and participation.

- Put into practice the four language skills (listening, speaking, reading, writing).

- Be interesting, motivating and close.

- Be adapted to the level and personal needs.

- Starting from familiar environments and then gradually dealing with more complex topics.
